Hey there! I'm Betty - a Chinese national with a Canadian citizenship hailing from Hong Kong and currently living in Los Angeles. In other words, I'm a 'third culture' kid who grew up exposed to a melting pot of cultures and experiences throughout my life. The adventure never stops.
On weekends, you'll either find me coffee shop hopping or building websites. There is an equally likely chance I'll also be eating something - not picky at all, I'll go from food trucks to drivethroughs to the fantastic DineLA restaurants in the city.
  Follow my appetite on instagram: @ooh.food

Full Stack Web Developer based in Los Angeles.

My journey with coding started with a budding interest during college. I've always considered myself a maker - the joy in building and creating returns a sense of reward that fulfills me personally and professionally.
I'm experienceed in the MEAN stack and working in a team setting. My latest project is a web app built for Laso Concepts. My experience also includes an app built for Honda Innovations in the 2017 AutoMobility LA Hackathon. See my works.

Graduated from UCLA in 2017 with a bachelor's degree in Economics, and minors in Accounting and Digital Humanities.

I have a comprehensive set of knowledge across econometrics, data mapping, media analysis, financial statements, and tax. I've also had work experience in these fields, specifically public accounting and marketing. Connect with me on LinkedIn to learn more about my work experience.
I enjoy multi-disciplinary learning so that I can fulfill both the logic and creative side of me. I enjoy practices which employ both sets of skills so that I can be a dynamic and versatile person.
