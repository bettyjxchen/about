Where To Study

Web App - Personal Project

Project Details

Project: Where To Study

Date: 2018


  • Where To Study is a personal project idea incepted from my frequent coffee shop runs. I loved to explore and study in coffee shops in LA, and got many questions from friends and family about the best spots in town. So, I decided to create this project to give my answers in the form of a web app.

  • MongoDB, Express, Angular, Node

    • 1. How to Use

    Where To Study allows you to explore the coffee shops by location. Simply pick a neighborhood you're curious about and a list of the best coffee shops will populate. Click to learn more about them!

    2. Filters

    Filter coffee shops by the categories that are important to you - wifi, parking, opens late, has outlets, etc. Tailor your study needs to finding the most suitable coffee shop when you are in list view.

    3. Detail

    Get all the details of your selected coffee shop, use the hours of operation, address, photos, to help you pick one to visit. Also, see how I personally like it - 4 to 5 stars means it is a pretty good study space.
